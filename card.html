Panda

Pandas are BIG eaters. Every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours, shifting up to 12 kilograms of bamboo!

  • Scientific name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average height: 1.35m
  • Average weight: 105kg
  • Lifespan: 30 years

Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears. In the wild, they are found in thick bamboo forests, high up in the mountains of central China. They are omnivores and bamboo represents the 99% of their diet.
